Prop_ID: A unique identifier for the property. The first character is a 
abbreviation of the borough,followed by a 3 digit number. Anything 
after the first 4 characters represents a subproperty.
    Boroughs:
    X - Bronx
    B - Brooklyn
    M - Manhattan
    Q - Queens
    R - Staten Island

Name: Name of the property

Location: Text location

Phone: Phone number of the property

Courts: Number of courts in the property

Indoor_Outdoor: Indoor or Outdoor tennis court

Tennis_Type: Tennis court surface type

Accessible: (Y)es or (N)o - wheelchair accessible

Info: Additional Information
